Chris Force is an artist and author based in Chicago.

He is the co-founder and publisher of Sixtysix magazine and the creative director for the creative consultancy Studio Sixtysix.

Chris's career began by documenting the 90's east coast punk scene. Armed with a notepad and a 35mm camera he cut and paste together his first magazine called ALARM.

His interest in writing, photography, art and design led him first to the Massachusetts College of Art & Design then to the School of the Art Institute of Chicago where he earned a Masters degree.

Selected Client list: Harley-Davidson, Bose, Porsche, Triumph, Ducati, Google, Royal Enfield, Rejuvenation, NeoCon, Le Creuset, Brizo, Koenigsegg, National Audobon Society, Revzilla, Shoei, New York School of Interior Design, Backpacker, HOG, American Iron, Business Insider, Common Tread, Jalopnik.
